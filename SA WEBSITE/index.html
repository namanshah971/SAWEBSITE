The Introduction

So in case you were wondering who we are ...

Sports Association acts as a bridge between 25 sports clubs and HKUSU.

OUR VISION

Publicise Sports Association and its activities amongst all the members of Hong Kong University Students’ Union

OUR AIM

Promote sports in HKU and to monitor and assist the affiliated 25 sports clubs in organising their events

OUR INTERESTS

Maintain a healthy relationship with HKUSU, Institute of Human Performance, 25 sports clubs, 10 faculties and 323 halls.

Affiliated Sports Clubs

The Sports Assocation is Affiliated with 25 Sports Clubs

Click on the links to find more about them!

Archery Club, HKUSU

Archery Club, HKUSU, founded in 1985, aims to promote the sport of archery and foster the spirit of sportsmanship.

Athletics Club, HKUSU

HKU Athletics Team is one of the largest university sports teams in HKU. Our club sincerely invites you to join it!

Badminton Club, HKUSU

The Badminton Club, HKUSU, one of the most traditional clubs of HKU, organises various events annually for its members and the team.

Basketball Club, HKUSU

The Basketball Club, HKUSU, provides a platform for all the HKU members to share their love for basketball.

Chinese Martial Arts Club, HKUSU

Honour to our society and the virtues we stand for. Glory to our spirit, the civil and martial. Join us to know more!

Dragon Boat Club, HKUSU

Founded in 2008, the Dragon Boat Club, HKUSU, is one of the youngest boat clubs in Hong Kong. Come and join us!

Fencing Club, HKUSU

If you wish to embark on your journey to become great, why settle for an average sport? Become a fencer!

Football Club, HKUSU

The Football Club, HKUSU, aims to promote football amongst the students of HKU. Come, See and Conquer!

Handball Club, HKUSU

Winning spirit, Solidarity and Perseverance - the three values shared by our club and the team. Become a part of us!

Hockey Club, HKUSU

Hockey is one of the three new ball games in HKU. Join us and get a chance to become the next Hockey Star!

Judo Club,
HKUSU

Founded in 1956, the Judo Club, HKUSU, aims to popularize and raise the level of the Judo Movement amd to strengthen foreign exchange!

Karate Club, HKUSU

As one the oldest associations of HKU, the Karate Club aims to provide opportunity to enhance one's physical fitness.

Kendo Club, HKUSU

Practising Kendo helps disciplining human character. We value human manner and honor, and molding our minds and bodies with training!

Lacrosse Club, HKUSU

The only University Lacrosse club in Hong Kong. Strength, speed and skills are the three major elements of this sweat(blood)-shedding game.

Netball Club, HKUSU

We aim at providing an opportunity for Netball players to continue to play the game in HKU, and at promoting the sport in HKU.

Rowing Club, HKUSU

Founded in 1981, HKU Rowing Club is the best and the oldest university boat club in Hong Kong. Together we row, like a champion.

Rugby Football Club, HKUSU

All are welcome, men and women, locals and internationals, freshmen and currents, beginners and big shots!

Softball Club, HKUSU

It is one of the new ball sport in HKU. The sport can train your brain and keep you physically fit at the same time!

Sport Climbing Club, HKUSU

Our Club organises numerous events for our members each year, ranging from climbing fun days, orientation series to competitions. Join us!

Squash Rackets Club, HKUSU

Our club aims at promoting squash game within HKU, as well as to provide a platform for HKU students to experience squash game.

Swimming Club, HKUSU

The Swimming Club, HKUSU, aims to promote swimming in HKU. Come, train with us at the Henry Fok swimming pool!

Table Tennis Club, HKUSU

The Table-Tennis Club, HKUSU, offers a chance to the members of HKU to enjoy and train in table-tennis. Come and join us!

Taekwondo Club, HKUSU

Ever wanted to practice Taekwondo to patch up as well as slim down? Here is your chance! Learn from the very basic!

Tennis Club, HKUSU

Please come and join us if you are interested and have passion to play tennis. Looking forward to meeting you all!

Volleyball Club, HKUSU
